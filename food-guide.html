Seafood Guide

If you only learn one thing here: eat the fish. Three spots, three kinds of traveler.

Giovanni's Shrimp Truck

Best for: backpackers and road-trippers

66-472 Kamehameha Hwy, Haleiwa, HI

The OG garlic shrimp truck. Need to get at least one plate whenever I'm back on island, and imo Giovanni's is the best. Good portions, picnic-table seating (just bring it to the beach to eat). Classic for an island round-trip lunch stop.

Ono Seafood

Best for: solo travelers and quick lunches

747 Kapahulu Ave, Honolulu, HI

A tiny counter that many locals call the best poke in Honolulu. Order the shoyu or spicy ahi, grab a seat outside, and enjoy.

Other spots I rate:

  • Morning Catch (Chinatown) — my personal favorite on island
  • Ocean's Taste — South Shore Market, Ward
  • Foodland Ala Moana poke counter — budget but still insanely good

Bonus hack: you can sample as many flavors as you want, so every time I go I make sure to grab a snack.
